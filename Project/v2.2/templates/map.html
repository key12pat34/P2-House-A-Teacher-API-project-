Project Summary

The purpose of this project is to compare houses for sale with schools in the area. This is useful for teachers seeking new positions to easily access information for schools nearby their desired relocation area. You can filter by numerous housing criteria and then see markers for available houses and nearby schools. This map could also be used for families considering a move to see what the best schooling and real estate options are in a given area.
